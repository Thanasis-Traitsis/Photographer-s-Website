who am i ?

My name's Marios, I'm 22 years old, and I live in Thessaloniki, Greece. Currently, I'm studying filmmaking and photography in my city. I also love graphic designing, and for the past 3 years, I designed a lot of digital artworks for Greek artists, some of them being among the most influential and popular in my country, such as FY, Rack, and Lil Barty. My artwork for FY's debut album "Lingo" is also featured on its Certified Gold plaque for its millions of digital streams. In these past years, I designed more than 200 single and album covers, and I directed and shot a few music videos for artists in my city.

my designing tools

I work with the Adobe Catalog, especially with Photoshop, Premiere Pro, and Photoshop Lightroom.
